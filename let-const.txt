1. var is globally-scoped and added as a property to the window.
Var can be reassigned and redeclared. let is block-scoped and can be reassigned, but not redeclared.
2. const is also block-scoped, but in contract to var can neither be reassigned or redeclared.
3. let can be reassigned, unlike const.
4. var is hoisted, meaning that var declarations are read first when compiled.
